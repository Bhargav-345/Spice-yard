Who We Are

A Kitchen Born in Mysuru

SpiceYard was born in 2012 in the heart of Mysuru, Karnataka — a city known for its palaces, silk, and extraordinary food culture. Our founder, inspired by his grandmother's cooking, wanted to bring those heirloom recipes to every table.

Every dish we serve is made from scratch using locally sourced ingredients, traditional stone-ground spices, and cooking methods that honour the Karnataka culinary tradition. No shortcuts, no compromises.
